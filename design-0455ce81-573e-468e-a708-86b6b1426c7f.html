Typography

Anton
Primary Headings • Bold • Condensed

A robust, condensed sans-serif that commands attention. Used exclusively for large, impactful headlines in uppercase, defining our strong, confident visual voice.

Quicksand
Body Copy • Rounded • Friendly

A display sans serif with rounded terminals. Provides a friendly, approachable counterpoint to the bold, structured Anton. Used for all body text, UI elements, and smaller subheadings.
